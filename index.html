Projects

End-to-end analytics, start to finish.

Each project was defined by a real business question, worked through data, and concluded with a decision-ready output.

Customer Churn & Revenue Leakage - Telecom

Challenge: Quantify the revenue risk from customer churn and identify which customers are most likely to leave.

Approach: EDA on 7,000+ telecom customers, logistic regression and feature importance analysis to pinpoint top churn drivers (contract type, tenure, high CLTV). Built a retention scoring model.

Results: 82% precision model enabling segment-level intervention; projected 18% churn reduction; $2.3M+ simulated annual revenue leakage quantified.

82% precision $2.3M leakage 18% churn ↓

🛍
Sales & Profit Analysis - Retail

Challenge: Understand where a multi-region retailer was losing profitability despite strong revenue.

Approach: Analyzed 9,994 transactions across 4 regions; isolated over-discounting in the Furniture category as the primary profit drag; mapped regional performance differentials.

Results: Uncovered 22% profit drag, identified the West as top revenue driver (+34% vs average), and revealed Q4 seasonality spikes to inform inventory planning.

Revenue Strategy - Urban Food Courts

Challenge: A Tier-2 city food court had a visitor-to-customer conversion rate stuck below 12%.

Approach: Funnel analysis, issue trees, and 5 customer personas to diagnose the conversion gap. Applied RICE framework to rank 5 low-cost interventions.

Results: Projected 25% conversion uplift within a 60-day rollout, validated against secondary market research.
